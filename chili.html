Ginger's Chili

Ingredients

  • One can of diced tomatoes
  • One can of pink beans
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • One half can of beer
  • One packet of taco mix

Directions

  1. Brown beef in a large pot

  2. Add diced tomatoes and pink beans, do not drain the beans

  3. Add half can of beer and taco mix, stir in

  4. Cover pot with lid and bring mixture boil

  5. Let simmer for 5 to 10 minutes more, then serve with favorite toppings
